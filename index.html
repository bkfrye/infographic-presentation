  1. Let's Learn About Interactive Infographics

    1. Interactive infographics are very powerful.

      Mainly, it causes your reader/user to engage with the infographic, which means they are spending more time with your content, and are interacting with it in a way that is very powerful for client brands.

    2. They can be simple or complex.

      There are a multitude of different possiblities when considering interactive functionality. Here are some common types typically found around the web:

      • Scrolling
      • User-driven data content (manipulation)
      • Animation
      • Multipagination (like this presentation)
